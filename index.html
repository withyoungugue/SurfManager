WHAT IS THIS?

SurfManager is a powerful session and data management tool designed specifically for AI coding applications. It provides developers with complete control over their development environment data.

Built with efficiency and safety in mind, SurfManager automates the tedious process of backing up, restoring, and managing application sessions - allowing you to focus on what matters: coding.
